About Me

Hello! I'm Dr. Sarah Johnson, a passionate mathematics educator with over 8 years of teaching experience at both university and high school levels.

I hold a PhD in Applied Mathematics from Stanford University and have dedicated my career to making complex mathematical concepts accessible and enjoyable for students of all levels. My teaching philosophy focuses on building strong foundational understanding while developing problem-solving skills that extend beyond the classroom.

I specialize in Calculus, Linear Algebra, Statistics, and have helped hundreds of students achieve their academic goals, from passing difficult exams to gaining admission to top universities.
